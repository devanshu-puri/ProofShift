Built “ProofShift” for the Google Antigravity Challenge 🚀

It transforms resumes, portfolios, and certificates into a Web3-inspired proof identity card.

The idea explores how Web2 profiles may evolve into user-owned, verifiable digital identities.

Built with Next.js, Tailwind, Framer Motion, and Gemini-inspired AI logic.
